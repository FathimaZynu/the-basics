Gardening Tools

Trowel

An essential tool for planting and digging small holes. Choose a sturdy, comfortable trowel for best results.

Pruners

Used for trimming and shaping plants. Invest in a high-quality pair for clean cuts and effective pruning.

Garden Fork

It is used for loosening, lifting and turning over soil in gardening and farming, and not to be confused with the pitchfork, a similar tined tool used for moving (or throwing) loose materials such as hay, straw, silage, and manure.

Seasonal Tips

  • Spring: Start planting early vegetables and prepare soil for the growing season.
  • Summer: Focus on watering and weeding. Harvest early crops and keep plants healthy.
  • Fall: Prepare your garden for winter. Plant fall crops and clean up garden beds.
  • Winter: Plan your garden for the next year and take care of indoor plants.
